Cicada is a design studio that works within the realms of design, research and social impact. We create spaces, objects and interactions that are meaningful and grounded.

Founded in 2015 ,the practice emerged as a friendship between us while travelling and documenting the built and natural landscapes around our hometown - Belgaum, in northern Karnataka. Our work is greatly informed and inspired by these influences and in that spirit, we strive for a design practice that is relevant to its time, place and people.

Team

Suyash Khanolkar

Partner

With a keen interest in earthen architecture and ecological planning, Suyash went to train under Didi Contractor in Himachal Pradesh in 2014 for a year.

While working with Cicada, he has been exploring his interest in clay and other natural materials through different mediums such as Terracotta pottery, natural plasters, vaults and domes construction.

Design and skill education through collaborative and hands-on learning is another area he has been experimenting in through workshops and volunteer programmes for student groups from design backgrounds, skill training institutions and makers spaces.

Mohit Yalgi

Partner

Mohit studied architecture at KLS Gogte Institute of Technology , where he graduated in 2014 obtaining a B.Arch. He trained at Samira Rathod Design Atelier during his academe.

Co-founding Cicada in 2015 with a strong desire to create a meaningful body of work inspired by the region of Belgaum, he continues to work on documentation and architecture projects in the region, bordering the states of Karnataka and Maharashtra. Between January 2019 to April 2020 he worked at Case Design in Mumbai , where he was associated with projects in Goa and Zanzibar, Tanzania.

Tanvi Dhond

Partner

Tanvi is a researcher and strategic designer with a focus on increasing social and economical mobility for under-served communities. Tanvi has a Bachelor’s degree in Architecture and a Master’s in Transdisciplinary Design from Parsons School of Design, where as a Part-Time faculty she taught classes on Service Design, Innovation studies and Strategic Design.

Currently Tanvi is a Designer at Dalberg Design, a development consultancy that works with Governments, NGOs and private sector organisations to solve some of the world’s toughest problems through design thinking. Tanvi is passionate about Human centered design and field research and uses the two to identify needs, create compelling narratives and build solutions for positive social impact.

Asmita Busgunde

Asmita hails from Raybag, in North Karnataka. She pursued her bachelor’s degree in Architecture at KLS Gogte Institute of Technology, Belagaum between the years 2009-2014.

During her internship she connected with a team of naturalists at Wildernest Nature Resort, Chorla Ghat and contributed to the design of their hospitality projects and went on to continue as their in-house Architect from 2013 to 2015. Keen on exploring steel and prefabricated structures, she has worked at Aequs SEZ, Belgaum - a company specializing in manufacturing and assembly for aerospace and allied industries, between the years 2016-2018. From February 2019 she has been associated with Cicada.

Vijay Patil

Vijay studied architecture at KLS Gogte Institute of Technology , where he graduated in 2018 obtaining a B.Arch. He trained at Hiren Patel Architects (Ahmedabad) and Studio 15,73 (Goa) during his internship. As an architect he has worked at Field Atelier in Goa and Thirdspace Architecture Studio in Belgaum, before joining Cicada in March 2020.

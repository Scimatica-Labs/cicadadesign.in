Cicada is a design studio that works within the realms of art, architecture, interior, product, education, research and social impact. Â We create spaces, objects and interactions that are meaningful and grounded.

Founded in 2015 ,the practice emerged as a friendship between us while travelling and documenting the built and natural landscapes of our hometown - Belgaum, in northern Karnataka. Our work is greatly informed and inspired by this immediate surrounding and in that spirit, we strive for a design practice that is relevant to its time, place and people.

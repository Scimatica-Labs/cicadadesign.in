The Orchard Pavilion

Navage, Karnataka
2015-2018

Amidst an orchard of Coconut, Mango and Jack-fruit trees, the structure is a simple wooden pavilion built to be used as a restaurant as well as an event space. With a large part of the picturesque and lush ambience created by the existing orchard, the pavilion is simply a space from which one can seamlessly engage with the surroundings, under the comfort and shade of a generous roof.

Constructed exclusively using Acacia (Acacia auriculiformis) wood in an effort to to use non-native and non- forest plantation timber, the structure is sheltered using corrugated sheets and country tiles and sits on a Basalt stone plinth, with the floor finished using IPS. A part of the connecting space accessed by a ramp, the reception, is built in adobe bricks finished with clay plaster.
